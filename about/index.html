About this website - GM Shaders is all about shaders for GameMaker! The goal of this site is to cover all of your shader needs with a complete shader overview and a comprehensive glossary designed specifically for GMS users.
Who I am - Hello! My name is Jon, but you can call me Xor. I started using GameMaker in 2010 and I've been working with shaders since 2013. I started xorshaders.weebly.com for shader tutorials while I was learning shader basics. It's time to take it to the next level!
What I'm up to - I'm almost always writing shaders for games, but I also write more shader tutorials and examples in my spare time.
